Local-first government workflows platform

Plan, review, and validate government paperwork without leaving your machine.

PigeonGov is a privacy-first CLI, TUI, and MCP product for US government forms. 34 workflows across tax, immigration, healthcare, education, benefits, veterans, legal, estate, identity, retirement, and more — all processed locally.
