What can it actually do?

Fill out any government form.

Guided, interactive workflows for tax returns, immigration packets, benefits applications, and more. Answer questions in plain language, get a validated bundle out.

Life happens. We help.

Major life changes trigger cascading government paperwork. PigeonGov covers 20 life events — tell it what happened and get a prioritized action plan with deadlines, workflow mappings, and the order to do things in.

$ pigeongov life-event marriage
life-event — marriage
$ pigeongov life-event marriage Life Event: Marriage Action Plan (5 workflows) ─── Phase 1: Right away ─── > identity/name-change Court petition + SSA + DMV + passport cascade > healthcare/aca-enrollment (!) [30 days] Marriage is a qualifying life event ─── Phase 2: Before tax season ─── > tax/1040 Switch to Married Filing Jointly (usually saves $$$) ─── Phase 3: When settled ─── > estate/basic-will Update beneficiaries and estate plan > immigration/family-visa-intake If applicable: spouse petition workflow Tip: Run workflows in order. Phase 1 deadlines are real.
