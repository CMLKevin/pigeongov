What can it actually do?

Fill out any government form.

Guided, interactive workflows for tax returns, immigration packets, benefits applications, and more. Answer questions in plain language, get a validated bundle out.

$ pigeongov fill tax/1040
fill — tax/1040
$ pigeongov fill tax/1040 --json Federal Individual Return (2025) Schedule 1, B, C, D & Form 8949 Filing Status: Married Filing Jointly Gross Income: $142,800.00 Adjustments: -$6,500.00 (HSA, IRA) AGI: $136,300.00 Deduction: -$29,200.00 (standard) Taxable: $107,100.00 Tax Calculation Federal: $16,290.00 State (CA): $5,847.00 SE Tax: $2,119.00 Credits: -$4,000.00 (child tax credit x2) Bundle saved to ./1040-bundle.json

Know what you qualify for.

Cross-program eligibility screening for SNAP, Medicaid, Section 8, WIC, LIHEAP, and SSDI. Uses federal poverty level calculations and program-specific rules. No signup required.

$ pigeongov screen benefits/snap
screen — benefits
$ pigeongov screen benefits/snap SNAP Eligibility Screening Household: 4 members | Monthly income: $2,800 Gross Income Test 130% FPL for 4: $3,467/mo Your income: $2,800/mo PASS (81% of threshold) Net Income Test 100% FPL for 4: $2,667/mo After deductions: $2,240/mo PASS (84% of threshold) Asset Test Limit: $2,750 | Yours: $1,200 PASS Result: LIKELY ELIGIBLE Est. monthly benefit: $459 Also check: benefits/medicaid benefits/wic benefits/liheap

Life happens. We help.

Major life changes trigger cascading government paperwork. Tell PigeonGov what happened and get a prioritized action plan with deadlines, workflow mappings, and the order to do things in.

$ pigeongov life-event marriage
life-event — marriage
$ pigeongov life-event marriage Life Event: Marriage Action Plan (5 workflows) ─── Phase 1: Right away ─── > identity/name-change Court petition + SSA + DMV + passport cascade > healthcare/aca-enrollment (!) [30 days] Marriage is a qualifying life event ─── Phase 2: Before tax season ─── > tax/1040 Switch to Married Filing Jointly (usually saves $$$) ─── Phase 3: When settled ─── > estate/basic-will Update beneficiaries and estate plan > immigration/family-visa-intake If applicable: spouse petition workflow Tip: Run workflows in order. Phase 1 deadlines are real.
